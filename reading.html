Enid Blyton

I have read various books and series of books by Enid Blyton. Her books feature adventures of kids or teens in which the solve mysteries.

The Mystery Series

Enid Blyton's Mystery Series features 5 kids Pip, Bets, Daisy, Larry, Fatty and Fatty's dog Buster solving mysteries in their village Peterswood. They and the village policeman Mr Goon race to solve mysteries first but The Five Find-Outers and Dog always seem to win. They've found stolen diamonds from a few spiteful letters that came to Mr Goon. Their pranks on the Mr Goon will make you laugh endlessly.

The books in the Enid Blyton Mystery Series include:

The Mystery of the Burnt Cottage

Someone has set fire to Mr Hick's cottage but who could it be? Fatty Larry Daisy Pip Bets and Buster the dog have their very first case to solve. But it's not easy being detectives with policeman Mr Goon telling them to "clear orf". The Find-Outers are determined - they have to solve the mystery before Mr Goon does!


I totally loved this book of the mystery series and it was totally great for the first volume of any series. In this book Mr Hick's cottage gets set on fire by someone (atleast that's what everyone thought) while he was away. The cottage contained valuable papers that were fortuanately insured.

The Mystery of the Dissapearing Cat

A prize winning Siamese Cat has been stolen and all evidence points to the Find-Outers' friend Luke. Fatty Larry Daisy Pip and Bets know Luke is innocent but how can they prove it? The Find-Outers have another mystery to solve!

Lady Candling's Siamese cat Dark Queen has been stolen. The clear suspect seems to be Luke but is there more behind the shadows, is the real culprit the very person who accused Luke. Well, its totally up to the Five Find-Outers and Dog to clear their friend Luke's name!

The Mystery of the Secret Room

Pip discovers a room in Miss Crump's old empty house isn't empty at all - someone has been using it! It's the perfect mystery for Fatty to put his new talent for disguises to use. Now it's up to Fatty and Pip along with Bets Larry Daisy and Buster the dog to find out who has been using Miss Crump's old house.
